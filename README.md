# Creative AI Tools: What Artists Need To Know
*Last Updated: September 23, 2025*

Hi, I'm Siddhi! My mission is to help artists better navigate the AI era.  

**Find me on:** [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@siddhisundar) | [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/siddhi.sundar) | [Newsletter](https://siddhisnewsletter.substack.com)  
Below is a comprehensive chart of 82 text, image, audio, video, and design tools with key details on privacy, data handling, ownership, and ethics. Updated each month to help creatives stay aware, empowered, and in control of their work when working with AI tools.  

**Key Caveats:** AI tool policies and features evolve rapidly due to legal, ethical, and technical changes. This chart is updated monthly based on available public information as of the last update date, but real-time shifts may occur, so always verify the latest terms directly on official websites or privacy centers before use. This is NOT intended as legal advice; use for informational purposes only to guide your research. Just trying to make artists' lives a little easier!

**Note on Transparency Labels:** Labels such as "Limited transparency" indicate that public information about training data, upload usage, or sharing policies is incomplete. This is often due to proprietary or legal constraints rather than companies intentionally withholding. Always verify directly with providers.

My perspective: there isn't a right or wrong answer here. Some artists I know choose to avoid these tools entirely; others don't mind and see it as contributing to the future of better technology. Zero judgment. The goal is simply to ask good questions and understand what's right for your own creative practice.

## September 2025 Update Highlights
- **New Tools Added**:
  - **Imagen 4 (Google)**: Advanced text-to-image generation with enhanced realism and detail, integrated into Google Cloud (GA in Gemini API and Google AI Studio as of August 15, 2025).
  - **Ray3 (Luma AI)**: Video generation model focused on high dynamic range (HDR) support for more vibrant outputs (launched September 18, 2025, with 16-bit HDR and Draft Mode).
  - **Krea AI**: AI design tool for generating and editing UI/UX designs with real-time collaboration features.
  - **Wan.Video**: Video generation tool specializing in short-form content with style transfer capabilities.
- **Key Privacy & Ownership Updates**:
  - **Gamma**: No training on user content for Team or Business plans; opt-out available for other plans via account settings (business data governed by agreements).
  - **Claude (Anthropic)**: As of late August 2025, consumer chats and code used for training unless opted out by September 28, 2025; opt-in data retained up to 5 years; commercial/API excluded.
  - **General Trend**: Increased emphasis on opt-out options and licensed data across tools due to ongoing legal developments (e.g., EU AI Act enforcement), but no major policy overhauls in September for most tools.
